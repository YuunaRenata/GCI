FUYUKI

A.D 2004

flame contaminated city

As tutorial, the fights are not overly difficult. Take advantage of the ease by looking out for strong Support Servants to add to your friend list. Jeanne d'Arc (tank and healer), Zhuge Liang (support), Lancelot (aoe), Heracles and Vlad III (nukes) are some of the Servants worth adding.

Orleans

A.D 1431

Hundred Years' War of the Evil Dragons

This chapter features a large and colorful cast of Guest Servants. Take advantage of the combat triangle by checking for enemy class tendencies in the top left of the planning screen. The difficulty quickly ramps up in this chapter, but with strong Support Servants and leveling your main lineup with the ember cards collected thus far, it should be quite survivable. Remember to use your Master Skills (mid right of screen), and in a pinch don't forget about those Command Seals (top right of screen).
